About Us

Nether Bakery

Welcome to Nether Bakery, where the aroma of freshly baked goods dances through the air, beckoning you to indulge in a world of exquisite flavors and textures. Nestled in the heart of Cianjur, our bakery is a cozy haven for those seeking the simple pleasures of life captured in every bite.

Our Vision and Mission

Our missions are:

  • Continuously develop high quality, nutritious and healthy products to adapt Indonesian taste.
  • Continuously open new stores to be accessible nationwide.
  • Continuously improve human resources quality by increasing welfare and competence to provide excellent service to customers.
